WELCOME TO AHLULKHEIR FOUNDATION

In 2015, the Ahlul Kheir Foundation emerged from the aspirations of a group of dedicated women who came together with minimal resources but a shared vision to significantly impact the lives of those in need.
Originally established as a community-based organization (CBO), their commitment and efforts gradually expanded over the years. By 2024, the organization had achieved official status as a non-governmental organization (NGO).
Today, the foundation is devoted to addressing the challenges of poverty, enhancing healthcare systems, and promoting educational opportunities throughout Northeastern Kenya and beyond.

Our Mission

Empowering marginalized communities through sustainable development by addressing food security, healthcare, education, and disaster relief. The mission focuses on building resilience, promoting self-reliance, and creating innovative, culturally inclusive solutions. The organization ensures swift and effective crisis response to support those in need.

Our Vision

A vision of a world where everyone has access to essential services like water, food, shelter, healthcare, sanitation, and education, ensuring a dignified life. The goal is to eliminate resource disparities, promote equal opportunities, and foster communities of shared prosperity and progress.

Our Values

Our values are centered around compassion, integrity, and collaboration. We are committed to helping those in need with honesty and transparency, while working together with others to create lasting impact. We focus on sustainable solutions and empower communities to thrive independently.

Community Borehole Water Project

AHLULKHEIR Foundation is preparing to drill a community borehole to provide clean, safe, and reliable water to families facing chronic water shortages. This project will improve health, reduce long walking distances, and support sustainable livelihoods for vulnerable communities.
