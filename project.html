Relief Efforts Gallery

Food Relief

Ahlul Kheir Foundation is deeply committed to supporting vulnerable communities by providing year-round food aid, with special emphasis on Ramadan. Partnering with Jamia Mosque, the foundation distributes nutritious food packages to hundreds of families so they can break their fast with dignity and comfort. Remarkably, as little as 30 dollars can feed a family for the entire month of Ramadan, covering essentials like rice, lentils, and dates.

Water Relief

The foundation has constructed shallow wells in drought‑prone regions, each supporting up to 400 people with clean water. In the past three years, at least four wells have been dug, benefiting thousands. Emergency tanker distribution further strengthens crisis response.

Construction Projects

Ahlul Kheir Foundation builds safe, dignified homes for vulnerable families and responds rapidly after disasters, restoring shelter, hope, and stability through community support.

AhlulKheir Madrasa Graduation Ceremony

We celebrate our madrasa graduates for their dedication to knowledge, faith, and personal growth, and pray their learning continues to guide and benefit their communities.

Health Aid

Through medical camps, medicines, and partnerships with clinics, the foundation delivers life‑saving healthcare to underserved families, building stronger and healthier communities.

AHLULKHEIR Foundation

AHLULKHEIR Foundation supports needy Muslim communities in northern Kenya by providing food, water, education, and shelter. Committed to creating lasting change, the organization fosters compassion and solidarity for a brighter future.
